Mini map-style tooltip (field name + farm/grower + GW/SO status lines only): add ?variant=mini or ?mapTooltip=mini (same as the weather map with agmri-weather.html?mapTooltip=mini). For Figma or screenshots with only the cards (no titles or notes), add ?cards-only or ?export=cards.
