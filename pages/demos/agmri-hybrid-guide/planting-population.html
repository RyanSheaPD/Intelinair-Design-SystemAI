1. Planting & population

All 9 hybrids in this lineup carry a >32K planting population recommendation per the InVISION® Seed Guide. Adjust by soil tier:

Soil tier Target population Rationale
High NCCPI ≥ 0.80 34,000 – 36,000 High-productivity ground supports higher populations; flex/semi-flex ears adjust kernel count.
Medium NCCPI 0.60–0.79 32,000 – 34,000 Standard recommendation; balanced ear fill.
Low NCCPI < 0.60 30,000 – 32,000 Lower populations reduce plant-to-plant competition on stressed ground.
💡
Tip: FS 6017V RIB and FS 6133VDG RIB are semi-flex ear types that compensate at lower populations with longer ears and better tip fill — strong fits for variable-stand situations on tougher ground.
⚠️
Note: DKC 66-06 and DKC 64-22 — confirm your DeKalb rep’s population guidance, as DeKalb hybrids may have different optimal population ranges than FS InVISION hybrids.
