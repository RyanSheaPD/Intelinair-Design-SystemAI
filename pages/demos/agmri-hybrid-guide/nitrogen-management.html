4. Nitrogen management

Trial data strongly supports managing nitrogen as a system, not a single application:

Strategy Trials Yield adv. ROI/ac
N as a system (inhibitors + split + supplemental products) 263 trials, 9 yr +8.8 bu/ac Consistent
2-pass vs 1-pass 124 trials +6.8 bu/ac $14.74
Product C (Eastern IL) 21 trials +2.2 bu/ac Supplemental
⚠️
Poorly drained fields (Fields E, I, Q) are highest risk for nitrogen loss via denitrification. A 2-pass system is especially valuable here — trial data show the response is “more notable in wet years when increased denitrification and leaching occur.”
💡
Product C (labeled rate, in-furrow or foliar V2–V4) can supplement a traditional program, but the Eastern IL response averages just +2.2 bu — treat it as insurance, not a primary N source.
