# Spring Wheat — Zadoks scale (14 stages)

**Intro**

- **14 stages**
- Zadoks scale begins at germination. Feekes scale begins at emergence.

Use this as the canonical copy for UI (cards, tables, tooltips). Feekes and GDD columns live in `pages/crop/wheat-growth-table.html`; card layout in `pages/crop/wheat-growth-stage-cards.html` (Spring · Zadoks tab).

| # | Growth phase | Zadoks | Description |
|---|----------------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Germination / Emergence | 00–09 | Seed germination occurs below the soil surface and the coleoptile begins emerging toward the soil surface. |
| 2 | Seedling Establishment | 10–13 | First leaves appear and the seedling becomes established. |
| 3 | Early Tillering | 20–22 | First tillers begin forming at the base of the plant. |
| 4 | Late Tillering | 23–29 | Tillers expand and canopy begins to develop. |
| 5 | Pseudostem Erection | 30 | Stem elongation begins as the plant prepares for reproductive growth. |
| 6 | Jointing | 31 | First node becomes visible above the soil surface. |
| 7 | Second Node | 32 | Stem elongation continues with additional nodes forming. |
| 8 | Flag Leaf Development | 37–39 | Flag leaf emerges, a key stage for yield potential. |
| 9 | Boot Stage | 41–49 | Developing head swells within the flag leaf sheath. |
| 10 | Heading | 50–59 | Wheat head begins emerging from the boot. |
| 11 | Flowering (Anthesis) | 60–69 | Pollination occurs as anthers emerge. |
| 12 | Early Grain Fill | 70–77 | Kernels begin filling during watery and milk stages. |
| 13 | Late Grain Fill | 80–87 | Grain transitions into the dough stage. |
| 14 | Ripening / Maturity | 90–99 | Grain dries and reaches physiological maturity. |

**Section groupings (cards UI)**

- **Early Growth** — stages 1–4  
- **Stem Extension** — stages 5–9  
- **Reproductive** — stages 10–11  
- **Grain Fill** — stages 12–14
